About Me

Hello! I’m Maryam Noori, a passionate and dedicated web designer with over 2 years of experience in crafting visually appealing, user-friendly websites. I specialize in responsive web design, creating digital experiences that are not only beautiful but also intuitive and accessible on all devices. My journey in web design started with a deep interest in technology and creativity. I have worked on a variety of projects, from personal portfolios to e-commerce platforms, and I am proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and popular frameworks like React and Node.js. I’m always eager to stay updated with the latest trends and advancements in web development. In addition to my technical skills, I’m passionate about creating user-centric designs that solve real-world problems. I focus on UI/UX design to ensure every project is not just functional but also enjoyable for users. Whether it’s a simple landing page or a complex web application, I am committed to delivering high-quality results that meet both client expectations and user needs. When I’m not designing, you can find me experimenting with new technologies, exploring creative design tools, or collaborating with like-minded professionals to bring innovative ideas to life. Feel free to explore my work below and let me know if you would like to collaborate!
